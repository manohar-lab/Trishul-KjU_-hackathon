The TRISHUL Solution

TRISHUL is a unified fraud detection platform that combines Graph Neural Networks (GNNs) and Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) to detect, explain, and prevent complex financial fraud in real time.

Why Traditional Systems Fail

Rule-based fraud systems analyze transactions in isolation. Modern fraud, however, is coordinated â€” involving multiple accounts, rapid micro-transactions, and hidden relationship patterns that traditional approaches fail to capture.

How TRISHUL Works

TRISHUL models the financial ecosystem as a dynamic graph, where accounts are nodes and transactions are edges. This allows the system to reason over relationships instead of single events.

Core Pipeline

1. Ingest
Real-time transaction streams are continuously ingested and converted into a graph structure representing account interactions.

2. Analyze
Graph Neural Networks analyze multi-hop relationships to identify coordinated fraud patterns such as fraud rings, account takeovers, and synthetic identities.

3. Explain
When suspicious behavior is detected, a RAG-based explanation engine enriches alerts with historical context and natural language reasoning, enabling faster analyst decisions.
