About TRISHUL

TRISHUL is an intelligent fraud detection prototype built to combat modern, coordinated financial fraud. Unlike traditional systems that analyze transactions in isolation, TRISHUL models the entire transaction ecosystem as a connected graph.

Our Motivation

Financial fraud has evolved beyond simple rule violations. Fraudsters operate in groups, distribute activity across multiple accounts, and exploit delays in detection systems. TRISHUL was designed to address this gap by focusing on relationships, patterns, and context.

Technology Behind TRISHUL

At its core, TRISHUL uses Graph Neural Networks (GNNs) to analyze transaction networks and uncover hidden multi-hop relationships that indicate coordinated fraud. To complement detection, Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) provides human-readable explanations enriched with historical context.
